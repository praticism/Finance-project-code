How it works

Your Business

There are no restrictions, people from every corner of the world can purchase goods through the card with a payment option of Mpay. We are accepting all currencies.

Mpay (Processor)

Mpay the Payment Processor validates the card information with the customer's bank. To ensure the money transfer.

Deposits Money

The transaction amount is debited from the customer's bank and is credited to the participant's Mpay account. i.e. you get money.
